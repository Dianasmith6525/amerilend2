# USA Loan Types & Required Documents

## 1. Personal Loans (Unsecured)
**Description:** Unsecured loans for general personal use, typically $500-$100,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID (Driver's License, Passport, or State ID)
- Proof of Income (Recent pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill, mortgage statement)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Employment Verification Letter (Optional but recommended)

**Typical Use Cases:** Debt consolidation, home improvements, emergencies, vacation, education

---

## 2. Installment Loans (Unsecured)
**Description:** Fixed-term loans with scheduled payments over time, typically $500-$100,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill or mortgage statement)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Previous Credit History (Optional)

**Typical Use Cases:** Large purchases, planned expenses, debt consolidation, major life events

---

## 3. Short-Term Loans (Payday/Cash Advance)
**Description:** Quick loans for immediate needs, typically $500-$5,000, repaid within 2-4 weeks.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Recent Pay Stub (Current pay period)
- Bank Account Information (Active checking account)
- Proof of Income (Employment verification)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Social Security Number (SSN)

**Typical Use Cases:** Emergency expenses, unexpected bills, emergency cash needs

---

## 4. Auto Loans (Secured)
**Description:** Secured loans for vehicle purchase, typically $5,000-$75,000, with the car as collateral.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid Driver's License
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Vehicle Information (VIN, make, model, year)
- Insurance Quote (If purchasing new vehicle)
- Trade-in Documentation (If applicable)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Employment Verification Letter (May be required)

**Typical Use Cases:** New or used vehicle purchase, vehicle refinancing

---

## 5. Home Equity Loans (Secured)
**Description:** Loans secured by home equity, typically $10,000-$500,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Tax Returns (Last 2 years)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Home Ownership (Mortgage statement, deed, property tax bill)
- Home Appraisal (May be required)
- Proof of Address
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Credit Report Authorization

**Typical Use Cases:** Home improvements, debt consolidation, major expenses, education

---

## 6. HELOC (Home Equity Line of Credit) (Secured)
**Description:** Revolving line of credit secured by home equity, typically $10,000-$500,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Tax Returns (Last 2 years)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Home Ownership (Mortgage statement, deed)
- Home Appraisal
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Social Security Number (SSN)

**Typical Use Cases:** Flexible spending, emergency funds, renovations, education, debt consolidation

---

## 7. Student Loans (Partially Secured)
**Description:** Loans for education expenses, federal or private, typically $5,500-$40,000/year.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Enrollment (Acceptance letter or enrollment verification)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- FAFSA Completion (Federal loans)
- Proof of Address
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Income (For parent PLUS loans)
- Tax Returns (For parent PLUS loans - last 2 years)

**Typical Use Cases:** Tuition, books, room & board, living expenses during school

---

## 8. Business Loans (Unsecured/Secured)
**Description:** Loans for business startup or expansion, typically $5,000-$500,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Business License
- Business Plan (For startups)
- Business Tax Returns (Last 2-3 years, for existing businesses)
- Personal Tax Returns (Last 2-3 years)
- Bank Statements (Business and personal - last 3 months)
- Proof of Ownership (Articles of incorporation, partnership agreement)
- Financial Statements (Balance sheet, income statement)
- Social Security Number (SSN) and EIN
- Collateral Documentation (If applicable)

**Typical Use Cases:** Business expansion, equipment purchase, working capital, startup funding

---

## 9. Debt Consolidation Loans (Unsecured)
**Description:** Loans to combine multiple debts into one payment, typically $5,000-$100,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- List of Debts to Consolidate (Credit card statements, loan documents)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Credit Report Authorization

**Typical Use Cases:** Credit card debt consolidation, multiple loan combination, debt management

---

## 10. Mortgage Loans (Secured)
**Description:** Long-term loans for home purchase, typically $50,000-$1,000,000+, 15-30 year term.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months, W2s - last 2 years)
- Tax Returns (Last 2-3 years)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Employment Verification Letter
- Credit Report Authorization
- Proof of Assets
- Purchase Agreement (For new home)
- Home Appraisal
- Title Search Documentation
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Proof of Address

**Typical Use Cases:** Home purchase, home refinancing

---

## 11. Private Money Loans (Secured/Unsecured)
**Description:** Short-term loans from private lenders, typically $5,000-$500,000, 6 months-3 years.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Recent pay stubs or tax returns)
- Bank Statements (Last 3 months)
- Proof of Address
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Business License (If self-employed)
- Collateral Documentation (If secured)

**Typical Use Cases:** Property investment, bridge loans, business ventures, short-term needs

---

## 12. Title Loans (Secured)
**Description:** Short-term loans secured by vehicle title, typically $100-$10,000, 15-30 days.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid Driver's License
- Vehicle Title (Clear or in process of being cleared)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Proof of Income (Optional)
- Vehicle Registration
- Insurance Proof
- Social Security Number (SSN)

**Typical Use Cases:** Emergency cash, quick funds, short-term needs

---

## 13. Credit Builder Loans
**Description:** Loans designed to build credit, typically $300-$3,000, secured by savings.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs or tax returns)
- Bank Statements (Proof of deposit capability)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Proof of Savings Account

**Typical Use Cases:** Credit building, establishing credit history, credit repair

---

## 14. Signature Loans (Unsecured)
**Description:** Unsecured personal loans based on credit signature, typically $2,000-$50,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs - last 2 months)
- Bank Statements (Last 2-3 months)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Employment Verification (Optional)

**Typical Use Cases:** Personal expenses, unexpected bills, debt consolidation

---

## 15. Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Loans
**Description:** Loans funded by individuals through online platforms, typically $2,000-$40,000.
**Documents Required:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of Income (Pay stubs or tax returns)
- Bank Statements (Last 3 months)
- Proof of Address (Utility bill)
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Employment Verification
- Purpose Description

**Typical Use Cases:** Debt consolidation, home improvement, wedding, vacation, medical expenses
